Our Mission

Why we exist

Coleman Integrated Solutions was founded on a simple observation: most organizations know they have compliance gaps — they just can't see them clearly enough to act on them. Manual audits are slow, point-in-time, and easy to game. Reports sit in drawers. Remediation stalls.

We built the Net Compliance Portal to change that. Continuous, automated visibility into your network device posture — scored, mapped to frameworks, and actionable from day one. No professional services retainer required to see your own data.

We're a growing team of engineers and compliance specialists, working across network infrastructure, cloud platforms, and security automation. Our clients range from regional financial institutions and healthcare networks to managed service providers running multi-tenant environments.

How We Work

Our approach

01

Outcomes, not outputs

Every engagement is scoped against measurable results — drift percentage, audit pass rate, time saved, devices covered. We don't deliver reports that sit in a drawer.

02

Code, not consultancy

Recommendations are only as good as the automation that enforces them. We write the playbooks, build the pipelines, and leave you with a system that runs — not a slide deck.

03

Direct, senior-level delivery

Every engagement is led by senior engineers — no hand-offs to junior staff mid-project, no account managers in the middle. The team you scope with is the team doing the work.

04

Fixed scope, no surprises

Engagements are clearly scoped with fixed deliverables. If something's out of scope, we say so upfront — not after you've already paid for three months of retainer.
